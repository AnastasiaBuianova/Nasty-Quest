Station 7: Part 1

The Maze

You have been spotted partying by the local natives and surprisingly they were really impressed by your (sick) dance moves. After chatting about your treasure hunt they decide to help you with it. They show you a mysterious temple within the jungle in which a holy ritual is hidden, which is the key to our next station. The entrance of the temple is a maze and marked with a red arrow.

As you find your way through the maze you collect parts of a key.
