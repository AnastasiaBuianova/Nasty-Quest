Station 2

Night

Your ship can travel not only around the World but also around the time. And the key you have found opens you the next portal full of danger. It looks like the portal is leading to the past. You go through and...
You are in the deep dark night. The place where you are is a camp. The night is quiet and beautiful but the people in the camp are not calm. These people haven't slept for many nights because of them. Camp people have fear. Do you feel it? There is some tension in the air, the smell of danger. Bang! You can't hide, you can't move, you have to abide. Bang! Panic. They are here again! They are undetected and unexpected! Bang! Their attack's been perfectly proved! They are raiders in the dark! Bang! You can't see them, you can't hear them, you can't follow them. They fly silently through the night!

Who are they?
