About Nasty Quest 2020

Scientific pirates

This year let's imagine that you are a researcher who travels around the World and through time on your super ship, solving challenging tasks in order to find the most wanted treasure (beer case)!


A quest is a set of tasks and questions that you have to solve in order to find something or whatever. There are thousands of different quest types. In the Nasty Quest you will be given 15 tasks in different scientific fields. You have to be creative, notice details and try to interpret them logically.


REWARD

The best team, which has the highest amount of points, will get a beer case as a reward.


Participation

You can go through the tasks alone or build a team of 2-3-4-5 people. You can also give your team a name, vote for a leader, or someone in your team may just say you that he/she is the leader, so be tolerant, also of a dictatorship.


Participating alone or as a team, you have to sign up yourselves, filling in the Application Form.

Main rules
if pirates may have rules

  • 1. You can google and search whatever you want, as long as you need it to solve the task. There is no limitation on using your computer except for obvious cheating and lying to your colleagues.

    Teams shown to be cheating are going to be disqualified.
  • 2. You have 2 tries to submit your answer.
  • 3. You may get 3 tips for every task. The first tip costs you nothing, the other 2 are -1 point (see SCORE AND TIMING).
  • 4. You have an opportunity to connect to your moderator any time via a video conference or chat. Your moderator is your good fairy, who wants to help you as much as he/she can! All the teams will be divided between these 5 cats randomly.

Score and timing

  • 3points

    go to Gryffindor if your team is the first one who solve the task right

  • 2points

    if your answer is right but you are not the first one or you have used 2 tips

  • 1points

    if it's not right but you had some right idea or you used 3 tips

  • 0points

    if none of your submitted answers were right or close to the right solution


Bonus points

  • +1 point If your solution is creative or you have used extraordinary ways to find the key.
  • Bonus task You will be given a bonus task, which might have to help you to increase your total score.

Timing

  • We expect it to be less than 4 hours . However, you will get your final results after all teams submit their last task.
  • On the 9th of May at 14:00 Berlin Time all the registered teams will get a link to an online video-conference, where you will be given these instructions again, have an opportunity to ask questions, say hello to the World, and you will get a link to the first task!
