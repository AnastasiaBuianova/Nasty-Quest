Station 3

Message

After celebrating the Victory in the Second World War your crew is doing all the possible, diplomatically navigating your ship away from the strong stream of the Cold War. It seems that you could make a time loop again, jump into home-time back. Russian brave Night Witches gave you the coordinates of your next station, where you have successfully arrived. Now you have an opportunity to work together with American researchers, whose work is full of mystery and open questions. Their computer instruments were able to reproduce this picture. They believe it is a message that some creatures sent. What does the message say and who are these mysterious creatures?

Help the researchers to find the answer to these questions because only this can help you to open the next door for your expedition.
